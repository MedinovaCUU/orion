3.3. Reaction rotor
The reaction rotor is the place where the sample is mixed with the reagent. The rotor (2) has 120 PMMA wells. The 
rotor is installed in a heating canal (3) thermostatted at 37 degrees C. The entire assembly is protected by a cover 
(1), to maintain the temperature inside it and prevent light from entering. The system functions continuously and 
for this purpose, it has a wash station (4) which empties and washes the rotor wells in 10 cycles. During each cycle, 
the analyser reads 68 wells with the optical system (5) that is built into the assembly.
