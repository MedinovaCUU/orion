4.8. Fluid control board – CIIM00053
This board is located at the top of the fluid zone.
The board controls the following elements, through a micro controller (U10):
• 
Stirrer driver (U18)
• 
Scales signal conditioning (U5 and U6) for the washing solution and high contamination waste
• 
Internal water and waste bottle buoy conditioning
• 
Driver for the wash station water heater (U1)
• 
Temperature sensor for controlling the thermostatting of the wash station water
• 
Wash station water dispensing driver 
• 
Wash station water dispensing start-up detection
