4.4. Tip detection board - CIIM00049
Board located in the upper part of the sample and reagent arms. These boards control the following elements, 
through a micro controller (U10):
• 
Fluid detection system
• 
Tip collision sensor
• 
The heating element and temperature sensor only in the reagent tips.
• 
Protection against ESD in the tip.
