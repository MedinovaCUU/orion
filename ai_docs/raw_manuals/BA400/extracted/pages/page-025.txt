The optical bench is secured directly to board CIIM00051 (42). The different leds (55) for each wavelength and the 
main and reference photodiodes (56) are welded to board CIIM00051 (42). The filters (53) for each wavelength 
are inserted in the filter holders (54) and screwed to the optical bench support (52). To ensure that the light beam 
for each wavelength hits the rotor (51) there are beam splitters (49) and lenses (50). The whole assembly is sealed 
by a rubber joint (48) and a cover (47).
