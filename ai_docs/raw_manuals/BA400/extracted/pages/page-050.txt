4.7. Rotor boards - CIIM00052
The analyser has two CIIM00052 boards which are located in the sample and reagent rotor assemblies. Boath boards 
have the same PCB but the fridge components have not been mounted on the sample rotor board. Each board has 
a different board selection switch code.
 
Elements that are common to both boards
• 
Micro controller (U5) 
• 
Cover sensor (U3)
• 
Control of fans located in the structure (U3 and U6)
