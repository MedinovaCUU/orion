4.3. Arm boards - CIIM00048
These boards are located on each of the five arms. 
The board has a micro controller (U1) which controls the drivers of the motors that move the arm vertically (U7) 
and horizontally (U8). 
It also contains the electronic control system of the start-up sensors for each motor.
