3.2. Reagent rotor
The reagent bottles are placed in the reaction rotor. There are two types of bottle, one containing 60 mL and one 
containing 20 mL. The rotor (3) has 2 rings. Only 20mL bottles can be placed in the outer ring, and both 20mL 
and 60mL bottles can be placed in the inner ring. The assembly has a barcode reader that reads the codes on the 
bottles placed in both rings. The whole assembly is refrigerated and has a separate power supply system to maintain 
the refrigeration when the appliance is turned off.
