3.8. Fluid connections
The entire fluidic system is mounted on a board located on the right-hand side of the analyser.
To facilitate the identification and monitoring of the fluidic system, the tube colour selection uses the following 
criterion:
• 
Blue: Distilled water tubes
• 
Red: Waste tubes
• 
Green: Washing solution tubes
