1. Introduction
In order for the imLD microscope to work correctly, we
recommend that the following preventive maintenance
operations be performed at least once a year.
2. Check of mechanical
components
a. Check the adjustment of the plate using the sample
positioning control knobs. The plate must remain in a fixed
position.
- If it has to be lubricated, proceed as follows:
- X axis:
- Clean the guides (1) of the bearing using a dry cloth. To
access the guides completely, move the plate forward
and back. Using a fine brush, apply silicone grease to
the bearing guides (1). In order to thoroughly distribute
the grease, move the plate. Wipe off any excess grease
using a cloth. Do not put grease on the teeth of the rack
and pinion.
- Y axis:
- Repeat the lubrication operation on the guides of the Y
bearing (2). Do not put grease on the teeth of the rack and
pinion.
