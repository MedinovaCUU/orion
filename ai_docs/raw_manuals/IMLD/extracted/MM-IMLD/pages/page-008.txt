3. Check of the optical
components
a. Check the integrity of the LED of the fluorescence mo-
dule.
- Remove one of the objectives, and select the empty
position of the revolver assembly.
- Turn on the fluorescence module. Put the potentiometer
at maximum.
- Put a sheet of white paper on the plate.
- Lower the carriage to the lowest position.
- Observe that the projected light matches the light in the
photograph.
- All the rectangles are very well defined, and the corona
that surrounds them, although diffused, is clean, meaning
that it shows no running or stains.
b. Check the halogen lamp.
- Clean if necessary. Use a cloth moistened with alcohol.
Avoid touching it with your fingers. Fingerprints or stains
reduce its useful life.
