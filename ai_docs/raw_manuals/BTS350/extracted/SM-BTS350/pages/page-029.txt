4.11. - Photometric
4.11.1. - Light intensity
This enables the user to know the current generated by the photodiode for a quantity of light received, thereby making it 
possible to know the light intensity of the instrument for each wavelength.
The semi-automatic analyzer reads the light intensity for each of the programmed wavelengths. This test can be done 
without cuvettes (light sensitivity to air) or with cuvettes. If using a flow cuvette, it must be filled with distilled water with 
a WASH cycle.
The light intensities read must be within the ranges indicated in the tables of Appendix I.1, if measuring the light intensity 
with the flow cuvette filled with water.
4.11.2. - Darkness
This makes it possible to verify the darkness current of the reading system.
Check that the housing and lid are well closed to prevent the light from entering.
