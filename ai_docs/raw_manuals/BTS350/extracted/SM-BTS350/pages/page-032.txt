4.12. - Open / Close QC tests
On leaving the factory memory positions 150 to 155 contain the tests used to perform the final quality control of the 
finish. These tests are de-activated in the factory; if they are activated again, the names of this tests will appear again in 
the list of programmed tests.
This option is for internal factory use and is of no interest to the Technical Assistance Service.
