4.9. - Cuvette temperature
To check the thermostatisation system operation, proceed as follows:
• 
Insert the temperature sensor of the digital thermometer into the cuvette holder.
• 
When requested by the programme to do so:
• 
Insert the test temperature.
• 
After five minutes of thermostatation, the programme will ask for a new test temperature. 
• 
Check that the temperature indicated by the digital thermometer is within the acceptance criteria.
